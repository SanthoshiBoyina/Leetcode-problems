Implement Merge Sort bookmark_borderGiven an array of size N, implement Merge sort.

Input Format
The first line of input contains an integer N - the size of an array. The second line contains the elements of the array.

Output Format
For each merge call of Merge Sort, print the array elements.

Constraints
1 <= N <= 20
1 <= A[i] <= 103

Example
Input
6
5 1 3 15 10 4

Output
1 5 3 15 10 4 
1 3 5 15 10 4 
1 3 5 10 15 4 
1 3 5 4 10 15 
1 3 4 5 10 15
